Get to Know Us Better

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation has built a robust network of volunteers and professionals working towards a happier, healthier society.

Who We Are

Licensed by the Central Government and headquartered in Chhattisgarh, our foundation operations maintain high-quality service and standard criteria. We believe that lasting social change starts at the grassroots level by directly empowering communities.

Every contribution we receive is directly allocated towards providing critical food support, women's entrepreneurship training, child education, animal rescue operations, and tree planting campaigns across multiple Indian states.

Our Core Initiatives

We drive change through six structured, ongoing projects, each designed to address a critical dimension of societal and environmental welfare.

Social Service

Project SEVA

Dedicated to alleviating hunger and poverty. Our volunteers distribute nutritious meals and ration kits daily to daily wagers, orphans, and homeless communities who face severe food insecurity.

Social Impact Reduces nutritional gaps and provides immediate survival security to marginalized families.
Skill Development

Project VIKAS

A specialized skill & career development program offering internships, practical work experience, and corporate mentorship to students and young graduates to enhance their national employability.

Social Impact Prepares local youth for successful entries into competitive technology, social, and finance sectors.
Women Empowerment

Project UDAAN

Uplifting women in rural and sub-urban spaces by training them in financial literacy, sewing, tailoring, handicraft creation, and entrepreneurship to secure independent livelihoods.

Social Impact Fosters financial independence, breaking generational poverty cycles and boosting household stability.
Child Education

Project BACHPANSHALA

Ensuring quality education for underprivileged children. We bridge literacy gaps by conducting free teaching camps, supplying school essentials, and establishing basic computer courses.

Social Impact Sparks a passion for learning, reducing child labor rates and dropout statistics in vulnerable areas.
Animal Welfare

Project JEEV

Compassionate care for street animals (dogs, cows, cats). Our teams provide regular feeding, establish makeshift shelters for weather protection, and coordinate local rescue operations.

Social Impact Fosters a community culture of empathy, reducing stray neglect and human-animal conflicts.
Environment

Project PRAKRITI

Focused on environmental conservation. We conduct extensive tree-planting drives, organize waste cleanup campaigns, and educate schools on sustainable zero-waste lifestyle practices.

Social Impact Combats local air pollution, builds green covers, and embeds ecological mindfulness in youths.
